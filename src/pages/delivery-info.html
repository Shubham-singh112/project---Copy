Delivery Information

We deliver across India — from metros to tier 3 cities. Every order includes free doorstep delivery and professional installation at no extra cost. Here's everything you need to know.

1

Order confirmed

You'll receive an email and SMS with your order ID and estimated delivery date within minutes of placing your order.

2

Packed & dispatched

Your furniture is carefully wrapped in protective packaging and handed to our logistics partner. You'll get a tracking link via SMS.

3

Out for delivery

Our logistics partner contacts you 24 hours before delivery to confirm your preferred time slot. Large items are delivered by a 2-person team.

4

Delivered & installed

Your furniture is carried to the room of your choice. Installation is completed the same day or scheduled for the next morning if delivery arrives late evening.
