How to initiate a return

1

Contact us

Call us or email with your order ID. You can also reach us via the contact form on our website.

2

Pickup scheduled

Our team schedules a free pickup within 48 hours. You'll receive an SMS with the date and time slot.

3

Quality check

Once the item is picked up and reaches our warehouse, we complete a quick quality inspection within 24 hours.

4

Refund processed

Your refund is initiated within 5–7 working days to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation email.

What qualifies

Eligible for return

  • Item is within 30 days of delivery date
  • Manufacturing defect or damage on arrival
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Missing parts or accessories
  • Significant colour or finish mismatch from listing
  • Item unused and in original packaging (change of mind)
Exceptions

Non-returnable items

  • Customised or made-to-order furniture
  • Items with visible signs of use, stains, or damage caused after delivery
  • Mattresses and bedding (hygiene policy)
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Sale items marked as "final sale" or "clearance"

Still unsure? Contact our team — we'll review your case and find the best solution.
