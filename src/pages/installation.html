Step by step

How installation works

1

Delivery arrives

Your furniture is delivered to your doorstep by our logistics partner. The delivery team brings all pieces inside your home.

2

Installation scheduled

If installation isn't done same-day, our team contacts you within 24 hours to schedule a convenient slot — morning or afternoon.

3

Assembly & setup

Our trained technicians assemble your furniture, position it exactly where you want, and ensure everything is level and secure.

4

Cleanup & walkthrough

All packaging is removed and disposed of. The team does a final walkthrough with you to make sure everything meets your expectations.

Included

What's covered

  • Full assembly of all furniture items
  • Positioning in your room of choice
  • Levelling and stability checks
  • Hardware tightening and quality check
  • Packaging removal and disposal
  • Post-install walkthrough with you
Good to know

Important notes

  • Installation is available in all cities we deliver to
  • Most items are installed in 30–60 minutes
  • Wall-mounting (shelves, mirrors) included free of charge
  • Electrical work (e.g. wiring for lights) is not included
  • Please ensure the room is accessible and clear of obstructions

Need to reschedule? Call us at least 12 hours before your slot and we'll arrange a new time — no fees.
